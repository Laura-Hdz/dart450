Why this book?

As an avid gamer, esports has become as important to me as what traditional sports teams are to avid sports fans. It has beome my motivation and goal to use my graphic design skills to help this industry grow and change the often-times narrow-minded view society has towards it.

As such, books that provide insight into the competition are always interesting to me, and I think are important eye-openers for people who may not understand what competitive gaming entails.
